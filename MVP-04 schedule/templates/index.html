Upload a screenshot of the rehearsal schedule. You'll brush over the dates/times you want, then we'll parse and export to .ics.

How it works
  1. Upload your schedule screenshot.
  2. Brush over (paint) the rows or cells you want to include.
  3. We mask the image to just your brush strokes and send to an OpenAI Vision model.
  4. Review and edit extracted events, then download a .ics.
